About Gucci

Progressive, influential and innovative, Gucci offers a radically modern take on fashion. Under the visionary leadership of Creative Director Alessandro Michele, he refurbished 21st century luxury and solidified its position as one of the world's most prestigious fashion houses. Versatile, contemporary and romantic, Gucci products embody the quintessential Italian craftsmanship and unrivaled quality and attention to detail. Gucci is part of the Kering Group, a global leader in apparel and accessories, with a range of major brands in the luxury, sportswear and designer brands.
